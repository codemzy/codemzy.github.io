About me.

I am a self taught web application developer and business owner based in Derbyshire, UK.

I have a first class degree in Management, and I have been developing websites for around 10 years, with experience in HTML and CSS. In 2015 I started to learn full-stack JavaScript, and haven't looked back since.

Currently, I am working through various courses to expand my front and backend JavaScript knowledge, and having a play with various frameworks.

Using technology, I enjoy helping businesses save time and money through online services and applications. I love writing code, front and backend, for work and for fun.
